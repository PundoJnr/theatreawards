Theatre Awards

About us

The Kenya Theatre awards are meant to honor outstanding productions, artistes and the legacy of practitioners who have become icons while encouraging and celebrating a blend of innovation and excellence in theatrical productions. By its structure and way of operations, the awards will also document and provide data and statistics of live stage theatrical performances held in Kenya in a given year with details of the venues, number of performances, Kenyan’s attending performances and where possible, the revenue generated from theatrical activities. A Jury of five people representing the theatre academia, practitioners, theatre associations and media will be tasked with watching performances between January and December of every year and there after hold a jury’s conference to evaluate and announce nominees of the awards. The nominees will then exposed to a public vote and the annual awards ceremony held on the 14th February of the subsequent year.
